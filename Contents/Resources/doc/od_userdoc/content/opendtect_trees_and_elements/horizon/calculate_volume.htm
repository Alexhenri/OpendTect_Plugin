3.7.9 Calculate Volume

Calculate volume: It is used to calculate the volume between the two horizons. The volume is calculated within an existing polygon. Select the polygon and press Estimate Volume button to calculate the volume within the polygon. To read more about this, please go to the chapter pointset: Pop-up Menus
