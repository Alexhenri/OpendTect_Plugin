5.4.2.4 Well-Tie Crosscheck Parameters

The cross-checking window is launched from the well ties display panel (Section- Well Tie: Display Panel) by clicking on the Display additional information button. The window contains the initial and estimated wavelet information. The wavelet can be computed between the two levels (start-end of data in time/depth or markers) that are provided at the top of this window. The window contains further key information: wavelets plot and the correlation coefficient. By changing the compute data between option, the correlation coefficient is auto-updated, this is done by using either Markers (Default) or Time/Depth. After achieving a high and positive correlation coefficient, the estimated wavelet can be saved. Importantly, the negative correlation coefficient shows that the polarity of the estimated wavelet is reversed. To avoid that the reference/initial wavelet's polarity has to be reversed.
