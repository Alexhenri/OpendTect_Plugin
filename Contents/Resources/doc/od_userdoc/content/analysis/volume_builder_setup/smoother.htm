5.2.3 Smoother

The smoother step is used to apply a three dimensional smoothing operator by specifying In-line, Cross-line and Vertical (ms) stepouts for 3D or two dimensional smoothing with Trace and Vertical (ms) stepouts in case of 2D.

Various operator shapes can be chosen (e.g. Hamming) and can be visualized by pressing on the View button. The CosTaper also requires specification of a "Taper Length (%)".
