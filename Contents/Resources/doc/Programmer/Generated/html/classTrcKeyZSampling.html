Detailed Description

Hor+Vert sampling in 3D surveys.

When slices are to be taken from a TrcKeyZSampling, they should be ordered as follows:

Dir Dim1 Dim2
Inl Crl Z
Crl Inl Z
Z Inl Crl

See also the direction() and dimension() free functions.
