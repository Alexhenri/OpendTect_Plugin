12.7 NN Slump Cube

This attribute set was created by Andrew Wilson of BG International for quickly mapping turbidite slumps. Slumps are characterized by chaotic reflection patterns and frequency losses; therefore, tops and bottoms are difficult to map using conventional techniques. In Figure 8 a conventional view of the seismic data (left) and slump detection results (right) is shown, and in Figure 9 the slump detection results are presented as a 3D body.
